Who am I

My name is Mitzi. I am a Tattoo artist for 10 years and do Tattoo training for the past 8 years for up and coming tattoo artist.

I’m pleasant bubbly very artistic artist. I travel to you do a one on one training in the skill of putting ink to skin.

My mission in life is to help and assist through proper training, other up and coming tattoo artist whom want to put there art to skin.

Available courses

Introduction Course

Are you interested in the art of tattoo but not sure if it is a career for you?
I can help you with this introduction course on tattooing to see if this is wright for you.
I will provide the training equipment for you.

Total Cost of the course : R 2500.00


What will you learn?

  • How to handle a tattoo machine.
  • How to tattoo.
  • How to learn more.

Requirements

Willingness to learn.
I will provide the equipment for you to use and to see if this is a career that you would like to pursue.


Basic Training Course

Want to peruse the career of an tattoo artist?
And you own your own tattoo kit.
Then this is the course for you.


Course Duration is five days

Total Cost of the course : R 5000.00

You will have four days of class.

  • How to interact with a client and layout of workplace.
  • How to keep yourself safe and the client with the right documents.
  • How to create a masterpiece from the drawing to the end product on the skin and many more.

Requirements

Willingness to learn.
Your own tattoo kit.
